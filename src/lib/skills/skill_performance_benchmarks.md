# SKILL CARD: Performance Benchmarks & Classification
**Load this when:** Analyzing influencer or campaign performance data.

---

## Engagement Rate Benchmarks
> Engagement Rate = (Likes + Comments) ÷ Followers × 100
> Look for this in: Campaign Tracker tab → `ER`, `Engagement Rate`, or `Engagement %` column

| Tier | Follower Range | Top Performer | Average | Under Performer |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Nano | 1K–10K | 8%+ | 4–8% | Below 3% |
| Micro | 10K–100K | 5%+ | 2–5% | Below 2% |
| Macro | 100K–1M | 3%+ | 1–3% | Below 1% |
| Mega | 1M+ | 1.5%+ | 0.5–1.5% | Below 0.5% |

---

## Performance Labels

### 🟢 Top Performer
- Engagement rate meets or exceeds tier benchmark
- Promo code redemptions or link clicks exceed campaign target
- High saves and shares on content
- Positive, on-topic comment sentiment
- CPE is below campaign average
- Content delivered on time, minimal revisions needed
- Audience demographics matched brand target

### 🟡 Average Performer
- Engagement rate is within benchmark range
- Conversions and clicks are on target but not outstanding
- Content delivered on time with minor issues
- Audience overlap is acceptable
- CPE is close to campaign average

### 🔴 Under Performer
- Engagement rate is below tier benchmark
- Low or no tracked conversions, clicks, or promo code usage
- High followers but low interaction (signals fake/inactive audience)
- Generic, spammy, or off-topic comments
- Content was late, off-brief, or needed major revisions
- CPE is significantly above campaign average
- Audience demographics didn't match brand target

## Audience Quality Signals
> Look for these in: Campaign Tracker or SOW tab → audience/demographic columns

| Signal | Healthy | Red Flag |
|---|---|---|
| Follower growth | Steady and organic | Sudden spikes |
| Comment quality | Specific, genuine | Generic or spam |
| Like-to-comment ratio | 10:1 to 20:1 | Very high likes, near-zero comments |
| Audience location | Matches brand market | Majority from unrelated regions |
| Engagement consistency | Stable across posts | Extreme highs and lows |

---

## ROI & Cost Benchmarks
> Look for these in: Dashboard tab → budget/spend columns

| Metric | Strong | Acceptable | Weak |
|---|---|---|---|
| CPE | Below $0.10 | $0.10–$0.30 | Above $0.50 |
| CPM | Below $5 | $5–$15 | Above $20 |
| CPA | Below target | At target | Above target |
| Conversion Rate | 3%+ | 1–3% | Below 1% |

---

## Influencer Reliability
> Look for these patterns in: Campaign Tracker → `Status`, `Deliverable`, and date columns

- **Reliable** — On-time delivery, followed brief, consistent engagement history
- **Inconsistent** — Mixed results, occasional delays or brief deviations
- **Unreliable** — Late or missing content, ignored guidelines, low engagement despite large audience
